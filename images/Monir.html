About

We are a small media startup, delivering a wide variety of engaging content in Stories format. We produce in-house content, collaborate with content creators, influencers, and synthesize other quality content into our stories format. These stories will be hosted on Monir, a platform where users can consume content posted by creators they follow. Users will also be able to look at content categorized under their “interests” such as specific movies, public figures, hobbies, lifestyle or news categories and the relevant top posts related to that interest will be there giving them a more immersive experience. All posts will be shareable through a link allowing people to view them in their browser on any device. Users will be able to repost content from their feed so that their followers can also see it. After a period of time, all users will be given an in app tool to create their own content to post.
