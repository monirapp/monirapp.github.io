About Monir

We believe full-screen tap-through vertical story format is the best way to view, create and share stories on mobile devices.

We are using Google’s open source AMP Story format as Monir’s underlying technology and will be customizing it to make it unique to our platform.

Our goal is to be the definite place where users can view these stories on a wide range of topics - world news, makeup tutorials, entertainment, food, fitness, DIY and how-to’s, fiction, etc.
